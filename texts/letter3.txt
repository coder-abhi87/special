1 june 2025



Aaj... usne haan kar diya.

Mujhe abhi bhi vishwas nahi ho raha. Woh ladki, jise maine Insta pe bas ek “Hi” bheja tha… jiske reply ne mujhe thoda awkward aur thoda hopeful bana diya tha… aaj usne mujhe accept kar liya.

Jab maine pehli baar usse dekha tha, toh bas uski aankhen hi yaad reh gayi thi mujhe.
Ek softness thi unme — jaise kisi ne dil ko haath se chhoo liya ho.
Aaj bhi woh aankhen waisi hi thi… lekin ab unme ek muskaan hai, mere liye.

Mujhe yaad hai, us pehli awkward si chat mein main bas soch raha tha ki kya usse fir kabhi baat ho bhi payegi…
aur aaj woh mere saath hai.

Jab usne “I Love you” kaha,  us moment pe sab kuch mute ho gaya tha.
Main us moment ko baar baar yaad rakhna chahta hoon.

Aaj main khud se ye likh raha hoon, kyunki shayad duniya ko nahi,
par mujhe yaad rahe ki jis ladki ke liye pehli baar maine dil se kuch mehsoos kiya tha,
usne ek din mujhe apna keh diya.

Shayad meri har line mein uske liye jazbaat chhupe hain…
lekin sabse zyada uske liye jo mehsoos hua,
woh tha — ek tarah ka sukoon.

Shayad ab yeh sirf ek DM nahi raha…
yeh ek kahani ban gayi hai — meri aur uski.

Aaj, uske haan ke baad, mujhe lag raha tha —
pehli baar zindagi thoda kam akeli lagi.
